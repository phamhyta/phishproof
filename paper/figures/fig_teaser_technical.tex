\caption{\toolname{} in one picture, expanded. Each evidence agent $m_i$ reads
the page $\page$, calls verification tools, and emits a label--evidence pair
$(y_i,\evset_i)$ over the shared schema $\Uschema$. Typed units
$u=(t,v)$ are assembled into the evidence graph $H(\page)$; majority-agreed nodes
form the consensus subgraph $\cons(\page)$, scored by evidence agreement $\agr$,
tool-groundedness $\grd$, and $\geascore=\agr\cdot\grd$. A delayed-oracle
calibrator $\kappa$ maps $\geascore$ to trust: high grounded agreement returns
$(\hat y,\hat\rho,\cons)$, while right-label, wrong-reason cases with label
agreement but weak or unverified evidence abstain.}
\Description{A left-to-right pipeline showing page observables sent to three
diverse evidence agents, tool-grounded typed evidence units assembled into a
shared evidence graph, consensus and grounded-agreement scoring, and the final
act or abstain decision.}
